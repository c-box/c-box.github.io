Boxi Cao 曹博希

I am a Ph.D. Candidate (from 2019.09) in the Chinese Information Processing Laboratory at the Institute of Software, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the Supervision of Professor Xianpei Han and Professor Le Sun. I received my Bachelor degree in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in June 2019. My research interests include:

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Interpretation of Language Models
  • Information Extraction
